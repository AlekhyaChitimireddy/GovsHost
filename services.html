Conference & Events

GOVSHOST Collaborates with Universities, Institutions, Organizations and other Associations with the theme of recent developments in the field of Engineering, Science & Technology for organizing International Conferences, Scientific events, Professional meetings & Summits and workshops. The main focus of us is to make peers and wizards meet to share their experience, knowledge and researchers by organizing Conferences, Meetings, Summits, Guest Lectures and Workshops. We have started its dedicated journey by organizing International Conferences and global standard events in different Institutions, Universities & conventions to provide scope for scientific interactions in the field of Engineering, Science & Technology to technically budding researchers from all across the world. Without discrimination among developing cities remote areas, Cosmo polis and cosmopolitan cities from all over the world. We organizes International Conference, Meetings, Summits at Universities booming at grass roots and find new researchers and innovators at grassroot level and to bring them in a global platform. Govshost focus on engaging academicians with rigorous interactions about updated scopes in various fields. IFERP interacts with its associates with theme of current technologies at conferences, scientific events, professionals meetings, workshops. Govshost will Collaborate with other Universities, Organizations and Associations for organizing Conferences, Summits, Meetings and Workshops.

Research Consultancy - Sustainable Development Goals (SDG)

Your Journey To Becoming A Prominent Research Specialist Begins Here!
Keen on spearheading the most pioneering research ventures of our time, in your field? Get in on the action by becoming a part of IFERP's Global Community. Here, you will be able to undertake and collaborate on groundbreaking exploratory pursuits to discover, analyze and implement the latest technology needed to achieve all SDG targets. Are you someone who wishes to make a difference in the world we live in? Then, working towards accomplishing all Sustainable Development Goals, which are a specific set of measures put forth to help - bring an end to global poverty, preserve and safeguard our planet, as well as, secure the welfare of all populations worldwide, is the best way to go about doing so!
